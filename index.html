BOARDROOM

Turn the TV On

Using the Touch Screen Controller:

  • Click Room Controls.
  • Click Displays.
  • Click the power button for the Boardroom TV.
  • Click Exit to return to the main menu.

Schedule a Meeting

Using Teams

Open the Teams app.

Schedule a new meeting, adding Boardroom as a participant.

  • Select Calendar.
  • Navigate to the desired date and time.
  • Press + New Meeting and configure the meeting title, start time, end time etc.
  • Add participants, including the Boardroom.

Using Outlook

Open the Outlook app.

Schedule a new meeting, adding Boardroom as a participant.

  • Select Calendar.
  • Navigate to the desired date and time.
  • Press New Event and configure the meeting title, start time, end time etc.
  • Enable the Teams Meeting option.
  • Add participants, including the Boardroom.

Join a Meeting from a Phone or Computer

Open the Teams or Outlook app.

Important! If you are physically in the Boardroom, mute both Mic and Speakers on your device.

Join the meeting:

  • Select Calendar.
  • Navigate to today's date and, find or select the meeting and click Join.
  • At this point, you can configure video and audio settings for your device. If you are physically in the Boardroom, before clicking Join now, mute both Mic and Speakers on your personal device
  • Click Join now.

Start an Ad Hoc Meeting in the Room

From the Touch Controller:

  • Click Meet Now
  • Click Invite someone and/or dial a number

Join a Scheduled Meeting from the Touch Controller

The day's schedule is displayed on the right hand side of the Touch Controller's home screen. Click Join to have the Boardroom join the meeting.

Note: The Join option doesn't appear until close to the meeting's start time.

Sharing to the Screen

When not in a Meeting

  • Turn on the TV using the Touch Screen Controller:
    • Click Room Controls.
    • Click Displays.
    • Click the power button for the Boardroom TV.
    • Click Exit to return to the main menu.
  • Click Share to display shared content. Note when sharing, the Share button becomes the Stop Sharing button.
  • By default, the TV is a Vivi display which can be accessed via the Vivi app. The room name is Admin-Boardroom.
  • It is also possible to connect via HDMI. There is a HDMI connection on the underside of the Teams touch screen controller. Plugging a device into this HDMI connection overrides the Vivi display.

When in a Meeting

  • By default, the Boardroom shares a Vivi display which can be accessed via the Vivi app. The room name is Admin-Boardroom.
  • It is also possible to connect via HDMI. There is a HDMI connection on the underside of the Teams touch screen controller. Plugging a device into this HDMI connection overrides the Vivi display.
    • Meeting participants can share content by clicking Share on the Teams meeting interface.

Troubleshooting

Room controls aren't working

Restart the computer using the Touch Controller:

  • Click the More button.
  • Click Restart Device.
  • If restarting the device does not resolve the issue, please submit a support request via email to the AV helpdesk.

Can't Join using a phone

  • Try disabling wifi on the phone and joining the meeting using a data connection.

Shared content does not display on the Boardroom TV

Check that the display is not set to People Only:

  • From the Touch Controller, click the Gallery button (on the bottom left when in a meeting).
  • Make sure Content + People is checked, not People Only.

Boardroom speakers are silent

Check the speakers aren't muted:

When participating in a call:

  • From the Touch Controller home screen.
  • Check that the speaker icon on the bottom of the screen isn't muted.
  • Press + or - a few times to check the level isn't very low (about 40 is good).
  • Click ... to expose more menu options.
  • Click Room Controls.
  • Select Audio.
  • Make sure the mute button is not highlighted.

When not participating in a call:

  • From the Touch Controller home screen.
  • Click Room Controls.
  • Select Audio.
  • Make sure the mute button is not highlighted.
